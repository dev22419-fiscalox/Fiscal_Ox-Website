    Inspiring Young Minds: A Tech Talk by Our Founders

    What happens when you mix curiosity, young minds, and technology? You get an EPIC interactive session filled with questions, ideas, and a vision for the future!
    Vicrum Vuppalapaty and I had an absolutely thrilling time engaging with the 6th and 7th graders at Navrachana Vidyani Vidyalaya, Vadodara. From discussing how technology is shaping our world to diving into real-world innovations, these students were on fire with their ideas!
    The energy in the room was infectious, and it was inspiring to see the next generation of tech leaders so eager to learn!


    A massive THANK YOU to Jinny Shah from Navrachana Vidyani Vidyalaya for making this happen and giving us the opportunity to spark curiosity and inspire young minds. It’s moments like these that remind us why we love what we do!
