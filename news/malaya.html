PRESS RELEASE June 2, 2025

Vicrum Vuppalapaty Ignites Entrepreneurial Spirit at Universiti Malaya’s Industry Forum on Machine Learning and Automation

Baroda - June 2, 2025 - Fiscal Ox is thrilled to share the resounding success of our CTO and Founder, Mr. Vicrum Vuppalapaty’s keynote at the Industry Forum on Machine Learning and Automation Trends, hosted by the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, Universiti Malaya on June 1st, 2025.

The forum, an international convergence of thought leaders from Siemens Healthineers, Bank Negara Malaysia, Security Centric Australia, and others, provided a platform to explore the future of AI, automation, and intelligent business systems. Representing Fiscal Ox and the Indian startup ecosystem, Mr. Vuppalapaty presented on “Transforming Customer Business Processes Through Cloud and AI Innovations,” captivating attendees with real-world insights from his entrepreneurial journey.

The interactive Q&A session emerged as a highlight of the event. Students posed insightful and practical questions such as:

  • “How did you develop your products from the ground up?”
  • “What was your starting point in building the business?”
  • “How did you plan your finances?”
  • “What research drove your innovation?”
  • “What is your honest take on AI and how it will shape our careers in tech?”

"AI is evolving, and so should we. We don’t have to be afraid of its presence. We have to grow with it. What will set us apart is our ability to understand customer pain points, stay aware of market trends, be practical, and build with empathy. Relevance comes not from coding alone, but from compassion-driven problem-solving."

Vicrum Vuppalapaty, Founder and CTO

Mr. Vicrum Vuppalapaty’s Message

His message resonated deeply with students and professionals alike, reinforcing Fiscal Ox’s commitment to building inclusive, purpose-driven technologies that meet the needs of both global and local markets.

Conclusion

The event underscored the value of cross-border dialogue and innovation, and Fiscal Ox looks forward to further collaborations with academic institutions and tech communities worldwide.
