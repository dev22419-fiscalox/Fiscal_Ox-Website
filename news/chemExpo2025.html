PRESS RELEASE April 29, 2025

Fiscal Ox Participates in ChemExpo Mumbai 2025 – Strengthening Connections Across the Chemical and Pharma Ecosystem

Mumbai, India — April 29, 2025 - Fiscal Ox Cloud Pvt Ltd, a pioneering cloud-based technology company specializing in LIMS and digital solutions for laboratories, manufacturing, and research organizations, proudly participated in ChemExpo Mumbai 2025, one of India’s premier trade exhibitions for the chemical and pharmaceutical sectors.

The event, held at the Bombay Exhibition Centre, offered an exceptional platform to interact with professionals and businesses from a wide spectrum of industries, including chemical processing, equipment manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and specialty chemicals.

Key Takeaways from ChemExpo Mumbai 2025:

  • Deeper Market Understanding: Engaging with a variety of stakeholders allowed the Fiscal Ox team to capture live insights into evolving operational challenges across chemical and pharma domains.
  • Customer-Centric Innovation: Discussions highlighted a rising demand for agile, customizable tools, particularly for laboratory digitization, compliance tracking, and operational automation.
  • Collaborative Possibilities: The event unveiled several promising partnership opportunities, paving the way for joint ventures, integrations, and strategic alliances.
  • Product Evolution Through Feedback: Real-time feedback on lab workflows and digital transformation challenges provided critical input for refining our LIMS and SaaS platforms.s
  • Validation of Vision: The enthusiastic reception of Fiscal Ox’s approach reinforced the company’s commitment to delivering smart, secure, and scalable cloud solutions for science-driven industries.

“We had an incredibly insightful experience at ChemExpo!”

Smit Senjaliya, Sales and Marketing Executive at Fiscal Ox

About Fiscal Ox Cloud Pvt Ltd

Fiscal Ox Cloud Pvt Ltd is a next-generation technology company focused on building AI-powered, cloud-native SaaS solutions for testing laboratories, CROs, pre-clinical research, manufacturing units, and HR operations. With an emphasis on compliance, customization, and innovation, Fiscal Ox empowers clients to digitize operations, drive efficiency, and achieve measurable business outcomes.
