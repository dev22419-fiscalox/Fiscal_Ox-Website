Vicrumnaug Vuppalapaty

Vicrumnaug Vuppalapaty is a young and dynamic tech entrepreneur, Founder, and CTO of Fiscal Ox Cloud Pvt Ltd, where he leads the development of innovative cloud-based solutions that empower businesses to thrive in an evolving market. With over 15 years of experience in AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and SaaS innovation, he has built a reputation as a strategic leader who transforms complex challenges into practical solutions.

Holding a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Colorado State University, Fort Collins, U.S.A., and a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Sathyabama University, Chennai, India, Vicrum combines deep technical expertise with strategic vision. His career spans impactful roles as a Technical Architect in the U.S., where he oversaw and led the development of over 50 private and state government cloud applications—many of which are still in active use today, including elicense.ohio.gov, IQVIA CTMS, IQVIA CRA-Central, IQVIA Engage, and Sparta DMS. His contributions have been pivotal in advancing cloud computing within the healthcare and laboratory testing industries for over seven years.

Beyond his impressive technical portfolio, Vicrumnaug is a passionate mentor, open-source advocate, and industry thought leader. He actively shares his knowledge at conferences, contributes to research, and nurtures the next generation of tech innovators. To him, technology is more than just a tool—it’s a catalyst for transformation, innovation, and meaningful change. His vision is to leverage technology to drive progress that benefits and nurtures humanity. Whether designing next-gen cloud solutions or inspiring future leaders, Vicrumnaug’s mission is clear: to push the boundaries of what’s possible and redefine the future of technology.
