FAQs

How much downtime should we expect?

Most workloads achieve near‑zero downtime using CDC and blue/green cutovers. Some require brief read‑only windows. We design to your SLAs.

Can you migrate heterogeneous databases?

Yes. We map types, refactor incompatible features, and validate with checksums and app‑level tests.

What about data sovereignty?

We deploy in‑region, enforce residency via policy, and document flows in the DPIA where required.
