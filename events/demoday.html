Fiscal Ox at Demo Day 2024 – Powering Innovation with Navrachana University

Fiscal Ox was proud to be invited to be a part of Demo Day 2024, an inspiring initiative by Navrachana University that celebrated the intersection of academic excellence and real-world innovation. More than a project showcase, the event stood as a testament to the university’s commitment to experiential learning—where students aren’t just graded on theoretical knowledge, but on their ability to build, collaborate, and solve real-world problems.

Our co-founders, Vicrum Vuppalapaty and Lekshmi Nair, had the honor of serving as judges at the event, alongside an enthusiastic gathering of industry leaders and educators. The Fiscal Ox team was also present to support the participants and witness their remarkable efforts. Over the past two months, students had worked diligently in teams of four, designing and developing innovative tech solutions and apps that tackled pressing challenges across various sectors.

Key highlights:

  • Fiscal Ox participated in Demo Day 2024 hosted by Navrachana University, a platform blending academic excellence with real-world innovation.
  • Co-founders Vicrum Vuppalapaty and Lekshmi Nair were honored to serve as judges, alongside other industry leaders and educators.
  • Over two months, students worked in teams of four to develop tech solutions and apps addressing real-world challenges.
  • The event featured functional prototypes, confident presentations, and thoughtful discussions from students.
  • Prizes were awarded for outstanding design and functionality, acknowledging student creativity and technical skills.
  • The event was brilliantly led by Dr. Ashish Jani and team, fostering a culture of innovation and talent development.
  • Demo Day 2024 highlighted the value of industry-academia collaboration and inspired confidence in the next generation of innovators.

From concept to execution, the energy and passion of the students were palpable. The event featured a wide range of functional prototypes, with students confidently presenting their solutions and engaging in thoughtful discussions. Their creativity and technical depth were recognized and rewarded with well-deserved prizes for outstanding design and functionality.

The event was brilliantly organized by Dr. Ashish Jani and his team, who curated an experience that not only encouraged innovation but also nurtured talent within the university. Demo Day 2024 reinforced our belief in fostering meaningful collaborations between academia and industry—and we’re excited to see where these young innovators go next.
