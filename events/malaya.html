Fiscal Ox at the Forefront of AI Innovation – Join the Conversation

We are delighted to share that our CTO and Founder, Mr. Vicrum Vuppalapaty, has been invited as a distinguished speaker at the Industry Forum on Machine Learning and Automation Trends, hosted by the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology, Universiti Malaya.

  • Date: 1st June 2025
  • Time: 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM IST
  • Platform: MS Teams (Online)

This esteemed forum brings together thought leaders and practitioners from across the globe to discuss cutting-edge developments in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and automation. Experts from Siemens Healthineers, Bank Negara Malaysia, Security Centric Australia, and other prominent institutions will be joining the panel.

Representing Fiscal Ox, Mr. Vicrum Vuppalapaty will present on the theme “Transforming Customer Business Processes Through Cloud and AI Innovations.”

His talk will explore the real-world impact of intelligent cloud platforms and the role of AI in streamlining enterprise operations, improving decision-making, and driving business value. With deep expertise in SaaS architecture and AI-driven transformation, Vicrum will offer insights into how emerging technologies are reshaping the future of work. This forum not only celebrates technological progress but also reaffirms our commitment at Fiscal Ox to building meaningful, scalable, and customer-centric innovations. We believe in the power of collaboration and knowledge exchange to accelerate change and open new possibilities.

We warmly invite professionals, researchers, students, and industry peers to attend the session and engage in this important dialogue. It’s a wonderful opportunity to connect, share perspectives, and explore avenues for future collaboration with Fiscal Ox.

Stay connected with us for event updates and post-session highlights.

For any inquiries or partnership interests, feel free to reach out to us.
